Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your personal information in connection with our Healthcare Risk Prediction System.

Your health data is sensitive. We implement industry-leading security practices and comply with all relevant healthcare data protection regulations.

Information We Collect
Personal Information
  • Account Data: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Health Assessment Data: Age, gender, residence, health indicators
  • Usage Data: Assessment history, results, preferences
Automatically Collected Information
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type
  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, interactions
  • IP Address & Location: General geographic information (country-level)
How We Use Your Information
  • Provide Services: Generate health risk assessments and personalized recommendations
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and model accuracy
  • Communication: Send important updates, security alerts, or customer support messages
  • Research: Anonymized, aggregated data for improving ML models and healthcare outcomes
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit; AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Database Security: SQLite with access controls and regular backups
  • Password Security: Passwords hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • Access Control: Authentication and authorization checks on all endpoints
  • Regular Audits: Security updates and vulnerability assessments
Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT share your personal health data with third parties without your explicit consent.

We may share non-identifying, aggregated data with:

  • Research Institutions: Anonymized data for improving healthcare models (with your consent)
  • Service Providers: Only when necessary for platform operations (hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or legal process
Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request and view your personal data anytime through your account
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications
Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Session Cookies: Maintain your login and user preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand how you use our platform
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraudulent activities

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Email: privacy@healthcareriskaprediction.com
  • Mail: Healthcare Risk Prediction Project, Healthcare Analytics Division
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days
