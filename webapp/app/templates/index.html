About This Platform

Our Healthcare Risk Prediction system represents cutting-edge machine learning applied to health risk assessment. Trained on 3,836 verified healthcare records, our hybrid Autoencoder + ANN architecture provides personalized risk predictions based on 12 key health indicators.

This platform demonstrates advanced techniques in unsupervised and supervised learning, combining feature extraction with predictive modeling for practical healthcare application. Ideal for MCA final year projects, healthcare analytics demonstrations, and research presentations.

Research-Backed: This platform implements peer-reviewed methodologies and demonstrates advanced ML concepts suitable for academic review and demonstration.
